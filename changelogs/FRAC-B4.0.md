# B4.0

## Changes
- Farmers Delight has been updated and has various tweaks, a notable change that you will notice is that the Cutting Board now holds a stack of items. Also a few new foods
- Vanilla water buckets now collect the same level of purity as other buckets 
- Added Undergarden Delight
- Melon, Pumpkin and Sugar Cane can be purchased with the Hot Hot Hot Catalog
- Added Virulen Mix and Void Fuel as Casting fuel sources
- Added Snow and Coke Coal compacting recipes
- Changed Energy Meter recipe
- Changed Undergarden catlog selling item to Gloomgourd Seeds
- Changed Void Fuel recipe to use more Virulent Mix
- Reduce time to craft Steel Ingots from Frost Steel ingots to 10 seconds from 30
- Added Iron -> Steel recipes in the Blast Furance
- Fixed Bread Selling
- Fixed Void Stone recipe using the wrong amount of fluid in its recipe
- Fixed crafted tools not being able to be leveled up (only effects new crafted tools)
- Banned salt mulches from Strainers
- Significantly increase experience needed for ultimine
- Removed block breaking experience for player skills 
- Healing now grants experience for player skills 
- Dealing damage now grants experience for player skills
- Slightly buffed eating experience for player skills
- Most foods (if tagged) will give experience for player skills
- Better foods will give higher amounts 
- Added Dump chests quest
- Fixed broken Invenotry Cable Connector recipe
- Removed iron, copper and emerald drops from some mobs
- Added Raw Clogrum and Froststeel block to block recipes
